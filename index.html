About Us

Who We Are

In business in Miami, Florida since 1955; Joe built Marine Tech from scratch into the thriving corporate concern it is today. With more than 15 employees and 3 buildings, MarineTech conducts business not only locally but also on an international level. Expanding into Boat industry, Marine Electronics, radars, GPS and Chart plotters, Audio and every type of Marine electronics; Marine Tech has become a direct dealer from top brands such as: Garmin, Jl Audio, KVH, Intellian, Furuno, Fusion, Furuno, Raymarine, Icom, Shakespeare and Simrad.

Not limited to the everyday boats Marine Tech will also do work on your Yachts, Boats and Personal watercrafts. Remembering his roots and strong work ethic, Joe says “We started out doing auto security and sound but, business grew quickly due to our quality products, competitive prices, excellent and honest installations.” Although the business has expanded exponentially, Marine Tech staff treats every customer as if they were the very first because we know how essential customer satisfaction has been for our success. Our offices are located at 3611 West Flagler Street in Miami
